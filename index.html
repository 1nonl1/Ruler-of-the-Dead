About the Game

Welcome to Ruler of the Dead, a thrilling Dungeon RPG game where you wake up in a mysterious world filled with creatures like Slimes, Ogres, and more. After being thrust into this strange realm, you must gather supplies, fight creatures, and grow stronger to survive. As you progress, you'll unlock new skills, weapons, and armor to aid you in your journey.

The game is still in its developmental stage, so expect updates and improvements. If you encounter any issues, please report them on the GitHub repository.

Game Lore

It was a normal day on Earth when everything changed. You were talking to your friend in a crowded city when chaos erupted. Someone attacked you, and you woke up in a strange, unfamiliar world. This is your new reality, filled with creatures and challenges. As you explore, you discover that defeating creatures makes you stronger, and you gain new skills and equipment to aid your survival.

Game Commands

Here are some essential commands to help you navigate the game:

  • use_item: Use an item or check its description.
  • scavenge: Search for food, with a chance of encountering entities or finding a chest.
  • rest: Recover health and save your progress.
  • check_stats: View your current stats.
  • view_inv: Check your inventory.
  • equip_item: Equip an item from your inventory.
  • show_equipItems: View your currently equipped items.

Note: You must rest to save your progress and advance to the next day!
